India's Bridge to

Catalyx Biotech was founded on a singular vision: to become the most trusted intermediary between India's world-class enzyme manufacturers and industries seeking premium-grade enzymatic solutions across the globe. We are not merely traders. We are formulation experts, sourcing specialists, and quality custodians. Whether you need 5 tonnes of custom-stabilized protease for food processing or a specialized cellulase blend for textile treatment — we engineer the solution.

HOW WE WORK

Our Formulation
& Supply Process

01

Requirement Analysis

Deep consultation to understand your application, process conditions, pH range, temperature stability, regulatory requirements, and volume needs. We speak your industry language.

02

Sourcing & Vendor Selection

We identify the optimal Indian manufacturer from our vetted network of GMP-certified facilities, comparing activity units, purity profiles, and certifications to match your spec exactly.

03

Bulk Procurement

We purchase in bulk (5–500 MT) to secure the best pricing, then hold inventory or forward purchase based on your delivery schedule. Full COA documentation provided.

04

Custom Formulation

In our formulation facility, we blend raw enzymes with stabilizers (maltodextrin, sorbitol, CaCl₂, glycerol), adjust activity to your target units, and optimize for shelf life and application performance.

05

Quality Control & Dispatch

Every batch undergoes rigorous QC — activity assays, microbial testing, heavy metals, and stability testing. Full documentation package for regulatory filing. Export in optimized cold-chain or ambient packing.
